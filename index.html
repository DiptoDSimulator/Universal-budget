Welcome to DC Budgeting!
Here's how to get started in 3 steps.
1
Set your budgets
Go to the Budgets tab → add your spending categories (Food, Transport, etc.) with monthly limits → tap Save.
2
Add your expenses
On the Overview tab, tap + Add entry on any category card. On mobile, use the green + button at the bottom.
3
Invite your team
Go to Budgets tab → copy your Invite Code → share it with your partner or team. They sign in and enter the code.
💡 Tap the Help tab anytime for the full user guide.
